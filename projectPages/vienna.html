AIS Vienna, with 800 students, is approximately 15,000 square meters divided amongst six floors with 17 various levels connected by a series of stairs and locker-lined corridors. This complex facility was expanded over the years, with various construction and expansion projects over the years, but sought out a 10-year Master Plan to identify solutions for improving curb appeal with the community and stay relevant in a competitive private school market. The campus' buildable footprint was maximized so alternative modernization projects were created with the goals of creating 21-century facilities, making the campus more energy efficient, and improving campus flow and function.
